Rule Status:

Hours

How Triggers Work

When the target asset reaches the defined threshold (e.g., crossing 1,000 recorded hours), the system will automatically generate a Pending Request.

Note: Multi-level approvals are out of scope. The generated request will be sent directly to the single designated manager for approval and conversion into a Work Order[cite: 32].
