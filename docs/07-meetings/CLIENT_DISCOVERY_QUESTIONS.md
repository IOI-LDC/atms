# Client Discovery Questions

## 1. Current Maintenance Workflow

What happens today when an asset needs maintenance, from the moment the issue is identified until the job is closed?

Ask separately for:

- Preventive maintenance
- Corrective maintenance

## 2. Usage Readings

How are operating hours, kilometers, or other usage readings captured today?

Follow-up:

- Who enters them?
- How often are they updated?
- What happens if readings are missing or wrong?

## 3. ERP Asset and Parts Data

How can the system access fixed assets and parts from ERP?

Options:

- API
- Database view
- Scheduled export
- Manual file export

Follow-up:

- What fields are available?
- Is there a stable ERP asset ID/code?
- Is there a stable ERP part ID/code?

## 4. Location Tracking

What physical locations should be tracked?

Examples:

- Warehouse
- Yard
- Maintenance yard
- Workshop
- Wellsite

Follow-up:

- Who is allowed to update asset location?
- Is location history required for all changes?

## 5. Preventive Maintenance Rules

What PM rules do they currently use?

Examples:

- Every 30 days
- Every 250 operating hours
- Every 10,000 km
- Whichever comes first

Follow-up:

- Should system-generated PM requests require manager approval?
- How early should PMs appear before they are due?

## 6. Work Order Closure

What information must be captured before closing a Work Order?

Examples:

- Final notes
- Parts used
- Updated readings
- Asset status
- Attachments/photos

## 7. Attachments

What documents need to be attached to assets or parts?

Examples:

- Manuals
- Datasheets
- Instructions
- Certificates
- Photos

## 8. User Roles

Who will use the system?

Examples:

- Maintenance Manager
- Technician
- Logistics
- Requester
- Administrator
- Viewer

Follow-up:

- Who approves requests?
- Who closes work orders?
- Who manages locations and dropdowns?
